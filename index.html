Who We Are

A Foundation Built on Compassion & Purpose

Founded on September 23, 2020 by Mr. Govind Shukla, InAmigos Foundation is a Section 8 non-profit organisation licensed by the Central Government of India, headquartered in Bilaspur, Chhattisgarh.

In a world often divided, InAmigos stands as a beacon of unity — addressing society's most pressing challenges through education, food security, women's empowerment, animal welfare, environmental conservation, and youth skill development.

With a growing network of volunteers, corporate partners, and thousands of interns, every initiative is designed for lasting, measurable change across underserved communities in India.

Section 8 Company 80G & 12A Certified CSR-1 Registered NITI Aayog NGO Darpan ISO 9001:2015

Our Core Focus Areas

  • Quality education for underprivileged children
  • Food distribution for the hungry & homeless
  • Women's empowerment & skill development
  • Animal rescue, welfare & feeding drives
  • Environmental sustainability & tree plantation
  • Youth skill training & employability programs
  • Clothing distribution for the underprivileged

Our Work on the Ground

Six Projects. One Mission.

📚

Education

Project Bachpanshala

Nurturing young minds by providing quality education to underprivileged children, creating spaces where happiness meets learning and every child is empowered with hope for a brighter future.

👗

Community Service

Project SEVA

Spreading warmth and hope through regular food distribution drives and clothing drives for the less fortunate, ensuring no one sleeps hungry or without warmth in our communities.

🌸

Women's Empowerment

Project Udaan

Helping women soar toward a brighter future through skill development workshops, vocational training, and financial independence programs that build confidence and self-reliance.

🌱

Environment

Project Prakriti

Healing the earth one sapling at a time — organising tree plantation drives, World Environment Day campaigns, and sustainability initiatives to combat deforestation and climate change.

🐾

Animal Welfare

Project Jeev

Empowering lives and spreading compassion through animal rescue missions, wildlife protection awareness, and regular feeding drives for stray and endangered animals across communities.

🚀

Skill Development

Project Vikas

Enhancing youth employability through professional skill training programs. Over 30,000 interns have been trained across India in digital skills, leadership, and career development.
